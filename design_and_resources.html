Design Tips

Color Mode

Always design in CMYK color mode for printing, not RGB which is for screens.

Bleed Area

Include 0.125" bleed on all sides to ensure no white edges after trimming.

Resolution

Use 300 DPI resolution for crisp, high-quality prints.

Fonts

Convert all text to outlines or embed fonts to avoid substitution issues.
